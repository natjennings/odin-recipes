Description

I have made parfaits for years. Yogurt, and fruit, fruit, and yogurt. Boring! No gains! This recipe has been evolving for a long time. Here's what we got so far. You can basically add in what you want and what you got, but there are some ~life hacks~ that we've compiled to make it better, so do what you can and get them gains baby. Greek yogurt, peanut butter, and mixed in protein powder are all good for protein. Adding fruit, granola, rainsins, and honey make it taste better, and the supplements like chia, flax, collagen, and creatine all help with nutrition.

Ingredients: Basic

  • Greek yogurt (Max protein)
  • Protein powder (we like Mycoprotein's whey stuff)
  • Peanut butter or other nut butter
  • Granola
  • Fruit (berries, peaches, banana, etc)
  • Honey

Ingredients: Advanced

  • Chia seeds
  • Flax seeds
  • Collagen
  • Creatine
  • Raisins

Steps

  1. Combine yogurt with protein powder, stir until homogenous
  2. Start adding your toppings. My favorites include the basic ones plus chia seeds and creatine
  3. Go workout!!
